About Us

Feed the City, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) organizaiton led by Executive Director Larry D. Coleman. Our non-discriminatory, community-based services are provided free of charge with no regards to a person's race, creed, age, or religious background.

Our mission is to assist those in need by providing food, clothing, and shelter in a stabilizing environment through physical resources and spirtitual encouragement.

We offer hot meals daily, emergency food boxes, and clothes to needy families.

Executive Director

Pastor Larry D. Coleman attended Southern Baptist Boyce Bible College for four years. He severed on the board of Prodigal Ministries as Program Coordinator; he also served on staff as an ordained Minister with the Evangel World Prayer. Pastor Coleman was the Executive Director of the Lords Kitchen and founded the prison ministry called Reconciliation Ministries. He is also the Founder and President of City Church International. Pastor Coleman is devoted to his wife and children.
